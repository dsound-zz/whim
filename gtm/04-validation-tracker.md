## Decision criteria (set BEFORE the calls — no moving the goalposts)

**✅ PROCEED to build** if **≥2 of ~6 calls** give a *concrete* paid commitment (verbal yes at ≥$200/mo **with a booked follow-up**) **OR** a clear, repeated single missing feature that unlocks payment.

**🟡 PIVOT** if there's strong pain but **price resistance is universal** → the wedge may be the white-label *widget* (higher WTP) not the raw API. Re-test with widget framing.

**🔴 KILL the B2B thesis (for now)** if **0/6** show current effort/spend on this problem, or willingness to pay anything. Stop building supply. Reconsider consumer-first or a different model.

**⚠️ SPECIAL FLAG — Legal provenance:** If **≥3 calls** name data licensing/scraping legality as a *dealbreaker*, the audit's #1 risk is confirmed. That outranks everything above: the roadmap becomes "legally clean sourcing" before any feature work.
